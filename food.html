Food and Nutrition Security

It is our mission to create a vibrant and healthier communities through nutrition, reducing child mortality especially the most vulnerable in rural areas. FRD Project; The Food Relief Drive project(FRD) is an initiative of the Evolving African leaders foundation in partnership with the Lagos state Ibile youth Aimed at bridging nutritional gaps in underserved rural communities especially in Lagos state, Nigeria.
