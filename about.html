About Us

We’ve had 3+ years of successful projects.

The non-governmental organization, EALF was particularly originated in May 2019, but officially registered in November 2022. EALF presently has its operational activities in Lagos Nigeria. The organization has executed several projects on Food and Nutrition Security, Education and Public Health. It is however, worthy of note that the organization would be addressing issues around several other sectors like Governance, livelihood, environment and climate change, technology, humanitarian and emergency support to mention but a few.
