Evolving African Leaders Foundation(EALF) is dedicated to improving the lives of individuals across Africa through Education, Health, Community Development and Others.

We bring positive change to Humanity

Evolving African Leaders Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable and underprivileged communities across Africa through strategic partnerships and impactful, sustainable initiatives.

our Organization overview

Thematic Areas

Our Areas of concern and responsibility

Food and Nutrition Security

It is our mission to create a vibrant and healthier communities through nutrition, reducing child mortality especially the most vulnerable in rural areas.

Education

EALF is dedicated to empowering students in secondary schools and higher institutions across the country through seminars, conferences, and skills-building initiatives that foster mental transformation and growth.

Public Health

Evolving African Leaders is dedicated to creating a malaria and typhoid-free future through dynamic partnerships and targeted interventions.

Humanitarian and Emergency Support

Evolving African Leaders Foundation is always at the forefront of humanitarian crisis response, delivering rapid emergency relief to affected individuals, communities, and regions

Testimonial

Mrs. Sholanke

AI am deeply grateful to the Evolving African Leaders Foundation for the Food Relief outreach that brought hope and sustenance to our community in Bariga. As one of the leaders of widows in our community, I witnessed firsthand the impact of this initiative on our families. The food packs provided were a godsend, and the thoughtfulness shown to us during this challenging time is truly appreciated. I pray that God continues to bless and guide the efforts of Evolving African Leaders Foundation as they strive to make a positive difference in the lives of the underprivileged.
