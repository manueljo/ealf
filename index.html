Launching the Fight Against Malaria

AYGF launched the Support for Malaria Elimination Project in Edo State, backed by the Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) and the National Malaria Elimination Programme (NMEP) to strengthen malaria prevention efforts. The event gathered key stakeholders, health professionals, and community leaders in the fight against malaria. Attendees included Edo State's Health Commissioner, Dr. Cyril Adams Oshiomole, Director of Public Health, Dr. Stevenson Ojeifo, AYGF’s Executive Director, Dr. Arome Salifu, representatives from the State Malaria Elimination Programme (SMEP), and traditional rulers. AYGF is committed to a malaria-free future through strong partnerships and innovative interventions.
